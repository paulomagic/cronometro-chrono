## Description

Keep time in sight without interrupting your work.

CHRONO HUD is a native Mac timer that stays above your other windows. Run a stopwatch, configure a countdown, or organize focused work with Pomodoro sessions.

FEATURES

• Always-visible floating HUD
• Stopwatch with lap recording
• Countdown with optional repeats
• Pomodoro focus, break, and cycle settings
• Transparency controls from 20% to 100%
• Compact mode
• Always-on-top and click-through modes
• Global shortcuts to show the HUD and toggle click-through
• Completion notifications and sound
• Collapsible event log with TXT export
• Local session history with CSV and JSON export
• Persistent themes, colors, and preferences
• Brazilian Portuguese and English interface

PRIVACY BY DEFAULT

CHRONO HUD requires no account, contains no advertising, and collects no data. Preferences, sessions, and history stay on your Mac. Export happens only when you choose a file.
